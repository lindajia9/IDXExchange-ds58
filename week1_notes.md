## Key Columns

**ListingKey**
- the primary key and uniquely identifies records in the Property resource

**ClosePrice**
- the final price the property actually sells for

**City**
- the city in listing address

**PostalCode**
- the postal code portion of a street or mailing address

**PropertyType**
- types of properties

**LivingArea**
- total livable area within the structure

**BedroomsTotal**
- The total number of bedrooms in the dwelling

**YearBuilt**
- the year the property was built
